Our Ministries

We believe every member has a gift to share. Our ministries provide opportunities to grow in faith, serve others, and build community together.

Children's & Pathfinder Clubs

Nurturing young hearts through faith-based activities, Bible adventures, and the Pathfinder programme. Our children's ministry helps kids discover God's love through fun, learning, and community.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to grow in their relationship with Christ through fellowship, worship, outreach, and Bible study. A safe and vibrant space for teens and young adults.

Music Ministry

Leading our congregation in praise and worship through song. Our choir, musicians, and praise teams use their gifts to glorify God and uplift spirits every Sabbath.

Health Ministry

Promoting whole-person wellness through health education, cooking classes, and community health events. We believe caring for our bodies is part of honouring God.

Women's Ministry

A supportive fellowship where women encourage one another in faith, prayer, and service. Through retreats, Bible studies, and outreach, we grow together in Christ.

Men's Ministry

Building men of faith through fellowship, accountability, Bible study, and community service. We encourage men to lead with integrity and serve with purpose.
